Vendor Feedback Document: Honeywell BGP+ Site Visits

Introduction

We appreciate Honeywell’s continued partnership and the efforts invested in recent site visits. The purpose of this document is to ensure alignment between reported outcomes and operational reality, and to drive improvements for future engagements.

Summary Table: Reported vs. Observed Reality

Key Findings

Salt Lake City:
- Cleanup and merge tasks were incomplete, requiring rollback to pre-visit backup.

Anacortes:
- Upgrade was not performed despite being in scope.
- Multiple critical features non-functional, contradicting ‘system healthy’ status.
- License recommendation was misaligned with actual system health.

Detroit:
- Persistent replication and sync issues despite reported upgrade completion.

Operational Impact

- Increased troubleshooting time and resource allocation.
- Delayed realization of benefits from BGP+ program.
- Risk to compliance and alarm management integrity.

Actionable Requests

- Root Cause Analysis: Detailed explanation for discrepancies at each site.
- Corrective Action Plan: Steps and timeline to resolve outstanding issues.
- Validation Protocol: Enhanced pre-visit and post-visit checks to confirm functionality.
- Training & Knowledge Transfer: Ensure Honeywell engineers understand replication and ACM architecture.
- Value Assurance: Align recommendations (e.g., licenses) with actual system health and needs.

Closing

We value Honeywell’s expertise and expect these improvements to uphold the premium nature of services provided. Our goal is to strengthen collaboration and ensure future engagements deliver consistent, reliable results.
