## Executive Summary

Marathon Petroleum, as a premium BGP Plus service contract holder, has experienced **significant service delivery challenges across three recent site visits** (Anacortes WA, Detroit MI, and Salt Lake City). These incidents represent opportunities to strengthen technical competency, pre-visit planning, and quality assurance processes that will enhance our long-term strategic partnership.

**Key Observations:**
- **Salt Lake City:** Cleanup/merge operations resulted in data integrity issues affecting 1,200+ SCADA tags
- **Anacortes:** System health assessment findings; planned software upgrade deferred from original timeline
- **Detroit:** M&R collection functionality issues; client patching completion targets not fully met

**Strategic Considerations:**
- Opportunity to optimize resource allocation and planning processes
- Enhancement of technical validation procedures for complex operations
- Acceleration of BGP Plus program value realization
- Strengthening of system change management protocols
